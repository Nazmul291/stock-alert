# Stock Alert — Marketing Video Script

**"Stock Alert — Never Run Out Without Knowing"** — ~75 seconds, Shopify App Store / social demo style

| # | Time | Visual | Voiceover / On-screen text |
|---|------|--------|----------------------------|
| 1 | 0:00–0:06 | Fast cuts: empty shelf icon, red "SOLD OUT" badge on a storefront, frustrated merchant | **VO:** "Every stockout is a sale you didn't even know you lost." |
| 2 | 0:06–0:12 | Shopify admin opens, Stock Alert dashboard fades in | **VO:** "Stock Alert watches your entire inventory — in real time." **On-screen:** "Live dashboard" |
| 3 | 0:12–0:22 | Zoom on a product card: badge flips from green "In Stock" → amber "Low Stock" → shows "~2 days left" | **VO:** "It doesn't just tell you what's low — it predicts when you'll actually run out, based on your real sales velocity." **On-screen:** "Stock-out prediction" |
| 4 | 0:22–0:32 | Split screen: phone buzzes with Slack alert, inbox gets email, webhook icon pings into Zapier, Klaviyo flow lights up | **VO:** "The moment stock gets critical, you're alerted — email, one-click Slack, Klaviyo, Shopify Flow, or straight into your own tools." **On-screen:** "Email · Slack · Klaviyo · Flow · Webhooks" |
| 5 | 0:32–0:44 | Storefront view: product goes to 0 → "Add to Cart" morphs into "Notify Me When Available" form → customer types email → button flips to "✓ Notified" | **VO:** "When a product sells out, your storefront automatically swaps in a 'Notify Me' button — no code, no manual work." **On-screen:** "Back-in-stock capture" |
| 6 | 0:44–0:54 | Inventory ticks back up on admin screen → product silently republishes → cut to customer's phone buzzing with "It's back in stock!" email | **VO:** "Restock happens, and Stock Alert takes it from there — republishing the product and notifying every customer who was waiting." **On-screen:** "Auto-republish + auto-notify" |
| 7 | 0:54–1:05 | Analytics dashboard: donut chart, 30-day bar chart, top products list animate in | **VO:** "And with built-in analytics, you'll always know what's trending toward trouble — before it costs you." **On-screen:** "30-day insights" |
| 8 | 1:05–1:15 | Logo + pricing card (Basic $3.99 / Pro $9.99, "30-day free trial") | **VO:** "Stock Alert. Install free, set up in minutes, never miss a restock again." **On-screen:** "Try free for 30 days — Stock Alert" |

## Notes for production

- Scenes 3, 5, 6 are the strongest hooks — lead with one of these if cutting for a 15–30s social version.
- Real screen recordings of the actual dashboard/products/back-in-stock pages will sell this better than mockups.
